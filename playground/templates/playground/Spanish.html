Welcome to the World of Spanish Language

About Spanish

Spanish, also known as español or castellano, is a Romance language that originated in the Iberian Peninsula and today has over 483 million native speakers in Spain, the Americas, and parts of Africa and Asia.

Spanish is the world's second-most spoken native language after Mandarin Chinese, and it is the world's fourth-most spoken language overall after English, Mandarin Chinese, and Hindi. It is also one of the six official languages of the United Nations.

Why Learn Spanish?

Learning Spanish opens up many doors for communication, travel, and business. It is the second-most studied language in the world, with millions of people learning it as a second language.

Spanish culture is rich and diverse, from the art of Picasso and Dalí to the music of Flamenco and the dances of Salsa and Tango. By learning Spanish, you gain access to a vast world of history, culture, and opportunity.
