Welcome to the World of English Language

About English

English, also known as English, is a West Germanic language that originated from the Anglo-Frisian dialects brought to Britain by Anglo-Saxon settlers in the mid-5th to 7th centuries AD. Today, it is the primary language of several countries and an official language in many more.

English is the most widely spoken language in the world, with over 1.5 billion people speaking it either as a first or second language. It is also the world's third-most spoken native language after Mandarin Chinese and Spanish.

Why Learn English?

Learning English opens up many doors for communication, travel, and business. It is the most studied language in the world, with millions of people learning it as a second language.

English culture is rich and diverse, from the works of Shakespeare and Dickens to the music of The Beatles and the films of Hollywood. By learning English, you gain access to a vast world of history, culture, and opportunity.
